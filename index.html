Services

We offer a wide range of services given below
  • Machine Embroidery
    Fine thread embroidery stitches on cotton T-shirt and Hoodies
  • Screen Printing
    Sharp and long lasting prints
  • Submlimation Printing
    Digital Printing on Sports Wear

Machine Embroidery

Customized embroidery of corporate logo on t-shirts, hoodies, formal shirts and caps. We do made to order custom machine embroidery on our products.

Screen Printing

Customize your t-shirts hoodies, polyester jerseys with your logo, text or image. We do custom screen printing - rubber printing, water base, pigment, High density, puffing and foiling on cotton as well as on polyester base fabric.

Submlimation Printing

Get infinite colors on your sports team jerseys. Get custom prints of your logo, name and numbers of your choice. We do digital sublimation printing on polyester dry fit material - Dot Knitted Fabric / Honeycomb / Nirmal Knits / Micro Polyester and Lacosta.
