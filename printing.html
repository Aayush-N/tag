The Tag Unlimited Printing Process

Printing

Create your custom T-shirts, Hoodies, Sweatshirts and Jackets online with premium silk screen printing on a 100% cotton fabric. Customize premium sweatshirts, pullovers, zippers, sports jerseys, jackets, formal shirts, collared t-shirts and caps in bulk.

Screen printing involves stencils to apply layers of ink on the garment. Each color in the artwork has a different stencil used for printing to achieve the final artwork. Color exposure and vibrancy will be perfect. If you can think of a color, we can print it.

Your print will be as good as the artwork. For screen printing we have set the minimum bulk order quantity to be 10 numbers of the same design. We provide free design assistance for any order with us. We assure quality product and delivery to your door steps.
